If you have powerful companions, they maypurchase equipment for you. Otherwise,equipment for novices is provided by theTemple of Shilen.
For now, you should seek to master the armsand armour you already possess. You willsometimes obtain arms and armor from themonsters you hunt. With some luck, you mayacquire cloth shoes, apprentice's shoes, or acloth cap.
The temple will provide you with two differentblades, a dagger and a squire's sword. Thedagger is swift, but its accuracy suffers, whilethe squire's sword (one-handed sword) isnoticeably slower than the dagger, but strikeswith greater accuracy. Remember that differentweapons may call upon different skills in theiruse.
